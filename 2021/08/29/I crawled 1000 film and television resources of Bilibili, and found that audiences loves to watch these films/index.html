Motivation and data sources

The motivation at the beginning was actually very simple. I remembered that when I was bored outside, I hoped to watch some videos to relieve my boredom, but I encountered the dilemma of no data many times. I wondered if I could crawl all the film and television resources of Bilibili to see which ones can be downloaded. Woolen cloth? (In the first two years, all film and television works can be downloaded, but with the strengthening of copyright awareness in China, more and more videos show: “Because of copyright restrictions, only online viewing”, even platinum member is just helpless.)

After having this idea, I feel that I have climbed all over again. Why don’t I find some data, including but not limited to the link of the video, qualifications (whether a large member is required), number of plays, country, video introduction, actors, video type , ratings, and our starting point: downloadable or not.

For the source of crawled data, if the title is: “I found out what movies my uncle likes to buy”, then crawling all the movies is the solution to the problem, but this is obviously unnecessary. So this article turns the focus to the top 380 movies, TV dramas and TV dramas with the highest number of broadcasts respectively. You could say that the most viewed movie may not be a good one, but it must be attractive enough. What are the conditions of the videos that attract viewers, what type of videos do users of Bilibili prefer, as a video sharing platform started with two-dimensional animation, whether Bilibili still maintains his “original intention”, these are all in this article. Want to find out the problem.

Movie

In the past few months, whether it is ghosts, miscellaneous talk, or even in the global Chinese debate competition a week ago, the debaters have demonstrated through the example of Huang Shilang “whether the appearance of the original god is the highlight moment of domestic games”, making “Let the Bullets Fly” This film, which has been released for more than ten years, has returned to the public’s field of vision again, and even has a tendency to break the circle (by the way, the debate competition is also very interesting, you can watch it if you are interested). It is not surprising to find that the variety of movies is blooming. It can be seen that the tastes of the audience are very mixed. Among them, plot, action, fantasy, and comedy have the largest audience, but in general, the distribution is quite balanced.

In the analysis of movies and distribution countries, I found that “Japan” or “China” are not the distribution countries for the most watched movies, and the first place is won by “United States”, which is actually a bit surprising to me, because regardless of the number of views No. 1 “Let the Bullets Fly”, or No. 2 “Your Name.” , not even a single American movie appeared in this entire top 10 list. From the popularity at the time of release, the “two-dimensional” characteristics of Bilibili and the reputation of the movie have attracted more Classic high-reputation American movies have more audiences, and the phenomenon that American movies have a high overall viewing volume but fail to hit the top ten on the list will appear.

TV drama

At the beginning, Bilibili occupied a place in the TV series through good exclusive Japanese drama resources, such as “Escape is Shameful But Useful”, “The Lonely Gourmet” and four famous novels. Here I have to mention the special area of ​​Bilibili, the ghost animal area, whether it is from Zhuge Liang a long time ago (“I have never seen such a brazen person”) to the “Da Tang Gang”, which is known as the treasure of the town station, Countless up masters in the ghost animal area have redistributed TV dramas decades ago for the second spring through tuning, filling in lyrics, and editing, arousing the enthusiasm of young people to revisit the classics.

And now we can’t even see a single Japanese drama in the top ten of the entire broadcast rankings. It is reasonable to speculate that Bilibili is now focusing on creating exclusive TV dramas, and the audience is more aimed at young people in their 20s. “The Sky of the Wind Dog”, “Sudden Vacation” and “Double Mirror” are all examples of the transformation to exclusive production and production.

(Why is the number of broadcasts so high? One is that the users of Bilibili match the audience of TV dramas, and the other is that the publicity is very strong.)

The genre of the TV series is similar to the movie, the only difference is that there are several elements unique to China —— “Military”, “Martial Arts”, “Spy War”.

Mainland China and Japan occupy 80% of the TV drama market at Bilibili, which also coincides with our above inference.

Among them, the types of Japanese TV dramas are not as diverse as those in mainland China. The most popular ones are mainly plot, urban, funny, and emotional. This also matches my impression of Japanese dramas. On the mainland, due to the relatively cheap copyrights of some old TV dramas, the types are more complex, and the audience may be more diverse (“The Hunting Ground”, “Iron Teeth Bronze Teeth Ji Xiaolan”, “Balala Little Devil”, etc. has more than 5 million plays).
